About US

We promote environmentally sustainable practices by engaging farmers in hands-on lessons, introducing them to sustainable agricultural techniques, composting and vertical landscaping.

West Bekaa is well-known for its wide green areas and agriculture, but there is a lack of awareness regarding the extreme use of fertilizers that causes damage to agriculture.Composting is a technique for the recycling and the decomposition of organic waste which, on the basis of biological transformation, from the fermentation of bio waste, produces a totally natural and high- quality fertilizer which can be used as a substitute for a chemical fertilizer.
